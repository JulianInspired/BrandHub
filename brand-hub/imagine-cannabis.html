Imagine Cannabis

Brand Identity Guidelines

Brand Overview

Imagine Cannabis is a creative, forward-thinking cannabis retail brand. It appeals to curious, open-minded consumers who see cannabis as part of a larger lifestyle of exploration and self-expression. The brand is modern, aspirational, and slightly unconventional.

Logo Usage

Imagine Cannabis
Imagine Cannabis
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "I" around the logo
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or alter logo proportions
  • Do not place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Monochrome versions are available for single-colour applications

Typography

Headlines — Light, airy, imaginative
Primary: Light weight / Used for headlines and feature text
Body text should feel clean, modern, and effortless to consume.
Body: Regular weight / Used for descriptions and general copy

Voice & Tone

Imaginative

Inspire curiosity. Invite customers to explore new perspectives.

Modern

Stay current. Reflect contemporary culture and design sensibilities.

Inclusive

Speak to everyone. Cannabis is for all walks of life.

Aspirational

Elevate the experience. Position cannabis as a lifestyle choice, not just a product.

Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Use the purple palette to maintain brand consistency
  • Lean into creative, editorial-style imagery
  • Keep messaging aspirational and forward-looking
  • Use plenty of whitespace in layouts

Don't

  • Use aggressive or pushy sales language
  • Clutter designs with excessive elements
  • Use outdated cannabis stereotypes
  • Stray from the approved colour system
