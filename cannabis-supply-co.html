Cannabis Supply Co.

Brand Identity Guidelines

Brand Overview

Cannabis Supply Co. is a straightforward, value-driven cannabis retail brand. It focuses on accessibility, transparency, and reliability. The brand appeals to practical, everyday consumers who want quality cannabis products without the fuss.

Logo Usage

Cannabis Supply Co.
Cannabis Supply Co.
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the cap-height around the logo
  • Do not alter, distort, or recolour the logo outside approved variants
  • Always use vector files for print; PNG for digital at minimum 2x resolution
  • Ensure sufficient contrast against all background colours

Typography

Headlines — Bold, direct, no-nonsense
Primary: Semi-Bold / Uppercase for headlines and signage
Body text should be clear, concise, and highly readable.
Body: Regular weight / Used for product details, descriptions, and informational copy

Voice & Tone

Straightforward

Say what you mean. No jargon, no fluff. Clear and direct communication.

Reliable

Be the brand customers trust. Consistency in messaging and experience.

Accessible

Cannabis for everyone. Remove barriers and welcome all customers.

Value-Focused

Lead with quality and affordability. Help customers make smart choices.

Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Use the green palette for brand consistency
  • Keep messaging practical and informative
  • Highlight value and transparency
  • Use clean, simple design layouts

Don't

  • Use overly trendy or flashy design elements
  • Make the brand feel exclusive or elitist
  • Use complex language or industry jargon
  • Sacrifice clarity for style
