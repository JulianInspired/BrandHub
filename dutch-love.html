Dutch Love

Brand Identity Guidelines

Brand Overview

Dutch Love is a welcoming, community-oriented cannabis retail brand. It blends warmth and approachability with a confident, knowledgeable voice. The brand celebrates the joy of cannabis culture and aims to make every customer feel at home.

Logo Usage

Dutch Love
Dutch Love
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "D" around the logo
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or alter logo proportions
  • Do not place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Use the primary full-color logo whenever possible

Typography

Headlines — Bold, warm, inviting
Primary: Bold weight / Used for headlines and key messaging
Body text should feel approachable and easy to read at any size.
Body: Regular weight / Used for descriptions, product info, and general copy

Voice & Tone

Warm

Speak like a knowledgeable friend — inviting, never condescending.

Community-Driven

Celebrate local culture and the people who make it vibrant.

Playful

Don't take things too seriously. A touch of humour goes a long way.

Knowledgeable

Be the trusted guide. Share expertise without being preachy.

Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Use the brand colour palette consistently
  • Keep messaging warm and approachable
  • Celebrate community and connection
  • Use high-quality lifestyle imagery

Don't

  • Use overly clinical or corporate language
  • Deviate from approved colour values
  • Use stock photography that feels generic
  • Make unsubstantiated health claims
