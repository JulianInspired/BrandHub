Muse Cannabis

Brand Identity Guidelines

Brand Overview

Muse Cannabis is a premium, refined cannabis retail brand. It positions cannabis as an elevated experience — artful, curated, and intentional. The brand speaks to discerning consumers who value quality, craftsmanship, and sophistication.

Logo Usage

Muse Cannabis

  • Maintain generous clear space around the logo at all times
  • Use the gold wordmark on dark backgrounds for maximum impact
  • Never place the logo over busy photography or patterns

Typography

Headlines — Elegant, refined, minimal
Primary: Light weight / Uppercase with wide tracking for headlines
Body text should feel polished and refined, with generous spacing.
Body: Regular weight / Used for product descriptions, editorial, and general copy

Voice & Tone

Refined

Speak with elegance. Every word should feel considered and intentional.

Curated

Quality over quantity. Emphasise the craft behind every product.

Artful

Draw parallels to art, design, and culture. Cannabis is a creative pursuit.

Understated

Let the product speak. Avoid hyperbole — confidence is quiet.

Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Use the gold and black palette for a premium feel
  • Keep layouts minimal with generous whitespace
  • Use high-end editorial photography
  • Let the design breathe — less is more

Don't

  • Use loud, busy, or cluttered design
  • Use discount-driven or bargain messaging
  • Use casual or overly informal language
  • Compromise on photography quality
